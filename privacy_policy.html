Privacy Policy

Odin.ga is a website that provides free online tools and resources for web developers. In this article, we will explain how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

What information do we collect?

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from you when you use our website. However, we may use third-party services such as Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about your usage of our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and other statistics. We use this data to improve our website and provide a better user experience for you.

How do we use your information?

We use your information only for the purposes of operating and improving our website. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We may disclose your information if required by law or if we believe that it is necessary to protect our rights or property.

How do we protect your information?

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. You are responsible for keeping your devices and browsers secure and updated.

What are your choices?

You can choose to disable cookies or opt out of Google Analytics by following the instructions on their respective websites. However, this may affect the functionality and performance of our website. You can also choose to stop using our website at any time.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes by posting a notice on our website or by email if you have subscribed to our newsletter. Your continued use of our website after the changes take effect will constitute your acceptance of the new policy.
